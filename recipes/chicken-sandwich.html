Homemade Chicken Sandwich

Just make it and eat it. you know you're gonna like it. Why waste my time?

Ingredients

  • water
  • milk
  • flour
  • 3 eggs
  • sugar
  • butter
  • active dry yeast
  • buttermilk
  • chicken
  • mayo
  • hot sauce
  • lemon juice

Bun Pun

Japanese Milk Paste

  1. mix 2tbsp water, 4tbspn milk, and 2 tbsp flour in skillet until gell like
  2. put it in plastic container with plastic foil cover

Bun Dough

  1. mix 320g bread flour, 1tsp sea salt, 2.5tbsp sugar in a mixing bowl
  2. microwave 1/2c of milk for 40sec
  3. add 1tbsp of yeast to the milk
  4. combine one egg with one egg yolk in a seperate container
  5. measure out 3tbsp of butter
  6. mix in order, the yeast with the flour, then add the paste, next egg, and finally butter
  7. put into a bowl and cover with a wet towel and wait 1-2 hours
  8. divide into 100g balls and roll until tension
  9. rest on a paper covered baking sheet, and under plastic for 1.5 hours
  10. preheat oven to 375 and eggwash buns with mix of a singel egg and a splash of milk
  11. cook until golden brown
  12. rub with butter and rest for 30min

Chicken

  1. mix butter milk with preffered seasonings and marinate chicken in milk in fridge fir 2-72hrs
  2. mix same seasoning as before with flour and MSG to create fried chicken batter
  3. fry chicken until golden brown and 165F

Sauce and Plating

  1. mix mayo with hot sauce, paprika, and lemon juice
  2. dry toast bun
  3. assemble sandwich with pickles and sacue on top of the chicken patty
