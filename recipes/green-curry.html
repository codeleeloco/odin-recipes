Pounding the Paste

  1. Toast seeds and peppercorn in a pan until fragrant
  2. Pound toasted seasoning in mortar and pestle until finely ground
  3. remove seasoning from mortar and pestle and place in seperate container
  4. Cut green chilli and put the in mortar and pestle. Use 3-10 per person depending on preffered spice
  5. Put chopped chilli, shallot, garlic, galangao, lime leaf, and corriander root into mortar and pestle.
  6. Slowly add seasoning as you pound. Add salt
  7. When almost done pounding add shrimp paste

Wok Hei ing It

  1. Put coconut milk in wok until milk seperates from oil. Or add oil if milk is not fresh
  2. Add curry paste
  3. Add chicken
  4. Add fish sauce
  5. Add preffered vegtable
  6. Add chilli powder, kaffir lime leaf, bamboo shoot
  7. Add chicken stock if dry
  8. Add palm sugar
  9. Add MSG
  10. Taste and adjust
