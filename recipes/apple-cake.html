Double-Apple Crumble Cake

Yummy yummy appy crummy. Yummy yummy in my tummy. Apples and sugar are the best things in the world. Now we added crispy breading too. Lick my balls and thank me when youre done baking this.

Ingredients

  • 3tbsp unsalted butter (1.5oz or 43g)
  • 4 medium pink lady apples, peeled, halved, cored, and cut into 1/4inch thich slices
  • butter
  • 2 cups all purpose flour (9.2 oz or 260g)
  • 1.5tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1.5tsp baking powder
  • 1/2tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup apple butter (220g)
  • 1 cup sugar (200g)
  • 1/2 cup creme fraiche or sour cream (120g)
  • 1/4 cup neutral oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2tsp vanilla extract
  • all-purpose crumble topping

Bake that shit

  1. Precook the Apples, heat 3tbsp of butter in a medium pan and stir apples for 10-15min
  2. Preheat the Oven and Prepare the Pan, preheat oven to 350. grease pan with butter and place parchment paper on bottom.
  3. mix the dry ingredients, in a large bowl mix the flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, amd salt
  4. Mix the Wet Ingredients, in a medium bowl mix the apple butter, sugar, creme fraiche, oil, eggs, and vanilla until smooth
  5. Mix the wet into the dry, make a well in the center of the flour to pour the mixture into. Mix from the center until well mixed
  6. Fold in apples, use a spatula to fold in the apples, leaving behind the excess liquid
  7. Put in pan and bake, Put batter in the pan and cover with the crumble. Bake until crumble browns and toothpick comes out the center clean. 1hr 20 to 1hr 30
